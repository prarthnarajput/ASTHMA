Asthma Symptoms

Shortness of Breath

Difficulty breathing during daily activities or exercise.

Wheezing

A whistling sound while breathing, especially when exhaling.

Chest Tightness

A feeling of pressure or tightness in the chest.

Persistent Cough

Frequent coughing, especially at night or early morning.

🌿 Asthma-Friendly Lifestyle Tips

Simple daily habits can help improve breathing and reduce asthma triggers.

🥗

Healthy Diet

Eat fruits, vegetables and foods rich in antioxidants to support lung health.

💧

Stay Hydrated

Drink enough water throughout the day to keep your airways moist.

🚭

Avoid Smoking

Stay away from smoking and second-hand smoke to prevent asthma flare-ups.

🏃

Regular Exercise

Light physical activities can strengthen lungs and improve breathing.

😴

Good Sleep

Proper sleep helps the body recover and supports respiratory health.

🌍

Monitor AQI

Check air quality regularly before going outdoors or exercising.

💙 Small healthy habits can make a big difference in managing asthma and improving quality of life.
